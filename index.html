Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this cost?

Contact us for more information about our application submission service for your shortlisted colleges.

Do I browse colleges on your website?

No. After you submit your details, our team personally researches and matches you to 5 VET colleges. You receive a detailed PDF via email within 24 hours with our recommendations based on your specific situation.

Why did the agent model change?

Education agents used to earn commissions when students transferred to new colleges, which created conflicts of interest. The government banned this model on March 31, 2026. We help you the same way agents did—matching you to colleges and handling paperwork—but YOU choose where to apply, and we don't earn commissions.

Have you really helped 2,866 students?

Yes. We've been helping international students with VET college applications since 2023. That number includes students we helped before and after the March 31 commission ban.

How long does it take?

You'll receive a PDF with your 5 matched colleges within 24 hours. Review it at your own pace. Once you confirm, we submit all applications within 48 hours.

What if I don't get accepted?

We match you to colleges where your profile is strong. Over 85% of our students receive at least 3 offers from their 5 applications.
